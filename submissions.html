(SUBMISSIONS)

We welcome submissions across contemporary art and photography.

(HOW TO SUBMIT)

We accept submissions sent in the form of low res digital pdfs. Please do not send submissions in the form of WeTransfer links as these will not be considered.

Owing to the large number of submissions we receive, unfortunately we can only respond to those projects we are keen to learn more about. Please accept our apologies for this. We will respond to projects we are interested in within three months of the submission window.
